About Me

Freelance Data Science enthusiast looking for new challenges. Interested in job positions in ML and NLP projects running on Python. I became fascinated by machine learning capabilities almost ten years ago at the university where I studied translation. I was busy teaching computer-assisted tools to translate the phrase 'Time flies' to my native language, trying not to get something related to the actual flying bugs but something more related to the context of the book. After completing my bachelor's degree, I joined Danske Bank and found success working with automation in Transaction Screening. Then, I finally decided to take up Python. Dedicating all my free time to learning, I headed for an IT career, where I landed a business analyst position and was responsible for payment engine compliance and its improvements. And then, after one eye-opening summer on a bicycle, I decided to change my job title from "analyst" to "scientist,' devoting my career to solving data-driven problems. Now, I am more than happy to say that I've spent my last year learning heaps from the Turing College community, where I've gotten to know the power of data.
